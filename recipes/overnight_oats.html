Overnight Oats

Description

Easy healthy breakfast that can be prepared in advance and can last for a week.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Greek or vegan yogurt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sweetener

Steps

  1. Combine oats and chia seeds
  2. Add greek yogurt and vanilla extract
  3. And then add sweetener of choice and milk
  4. Cover with a lid and chill in the fridge overnight
