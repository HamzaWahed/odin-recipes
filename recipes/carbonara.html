Carbonara

Description

Humble ingredients—eggs, noodles, cheese, and pork—combine to create glossy, glorious pasta carbonara. It's the no-food-in-the-house dinner of our dreams.

Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp. kosher salt
  • 4 oz guanciale
  • 2 oz parmesan
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 2 large eggs
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 lb spaghetti

Steps

  1. Heat the water
  2. Cut the guanciale into strips
  3. Whisk 4 egg yolks and 2 eggs
  4. Add oil and cook guanciale
  5. Cook the pasta
  6. Add the egg mixture and guanciale
